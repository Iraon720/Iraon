My Top 3 Hobbies

Ride Motorcyle

Growing up in the small country town of Willacoochee, Ga we had to create our own fun outside of playing video games. I grew up watching my uncles and cousins race their bikes against each other or people from out of town. I started learning how to operate all types of vehicles early on from dirt bikes,cars,four-wheelers, big trucks, and buses.When I reached highschool my uncles finally allowed me to ride their motorcyles and I became addicted.

My junior year of highschool I had my first wreck. You would think that would stop me from riding, but it didn't it made me want to ride even more. Even after all the doctor appointments and meeting with a plastic surgeon I coudln't wait to ride again.It is probably my second favorite hobby because of how peaceful and free I feel when I ride. You have nothing to do but think, and enjoy how beautiful earth is.

Motorcyle Brands I Have Rode:

  • Suzuki Hayabusa
  • Suzuki GXSR 1000
  • Suzuki GXSR 750
  • Kawasaki Ninja 600
  • Harley Davidson Road Glide

Gym

Growing up I really loved sports. Me and my cousins would play football, basketball, or baseball everyday no matter the weather. When we started playing sports for our middle school, we were required to go lift weights to prepare for highschool. Instead of it becoming something I had to do, it became something I really wanted to do.

I loved how my body was transforming being that I was a greedy fat kid who loved eating. Once I graduated highschool I stopped going for the first 2 years of college. Once I started back going I realized working out was a great way to relieve stress and plus I was just partying and eating so I had gained all that weight back. Now it is one of my main hobbies, no matter where I am or how busy I am I have to go the gym for at least an hour a day.I love getting stronger, releiving stress, and the pain that I feel after a great workout.

Favorite Workouts:

  1. Decline Bench Press
  2. Calf Raises
  3. Hammer Curls
  4. Cable Press
  5. Tricep Pulldowns
  6. DB Bench Press
  7. Leg Extensions
  8. Tricep Dips
  9. Forearm Twists
  10. Lat Pulldowns

Video Games

As a kid growing up in the country, another thing to do for fun was playing video games. There were so many types of games and consoles out that almost every one I knew had one. As a kid playing games were just for fun,and to keep your attention. When I got older I noticed that playing games were not just only fun but yet another great way to decompress from everything going on.

It became even better over the years because of the ability to play with friends online and also cross play with different consoles. Another reason I love playing is because they make games for different movies, popular anime, and books.

5 Games I Play Right Now:

  1. NBA 2k23
  2. Spiderman: Miles Morales
  3. Call of Duty MW2
  4. Fortnite
  5. God of War:Ragnorok
