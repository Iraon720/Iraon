My Favorite Hobbies!

Ride Motorcyle

Growing up in the small country town of Willacoochee, Ga we had to create our own fun outside of playing video games. I grew up watching my uncles and cousins race their bikes against each other or people from out of town. I started learning how to operate all types of vehicles early on from dirt bikes,cars,four-wheelers, big trucks, and buses.When I reached highschool my uncles finally allowed me to ride their motorcyles

Gym

Growing up I really loved sports. Me and my cousins would play football, basketball, or baseball everyday no matter the weather. When we started playing sports for our middle school, we were required to go lift weights to prepare for highschool. Instead of it becoming something I had to do, it became something I really wanted to do. I loved how my body was transforming being that I was a greedy fat kid who loved eating. Once I graduated highschool I stopped going for the first 2 years of college. Once I started back going I realized working out was a great way to relieve stress and plus I was just partying and eating so I had gained all that weight back. Now it is one of my main hobbies, no matter where I am or how busy I am I have to go the gym for at least an hour a day.I love getting stronger, releiving stress, and the pain that I feel after a great workout.
